    Welcome to CookCraft

    Here, you can easily calculate your optimal calorie intake, create a personalized collection of your favorite recipes, and plan your daily menu with exciting and delicious dishes.

    Follow these simple steps to get started:

    1. Search for recipes by ingredients, cuisine or dietary preferences to find the perfect meal.
    2. Add your favorite recipes to your collection by clicking the bookmark icon, then access them anytime via the 'Bookmarks' link in the navigation menu.
    3. Want a personalized menu? Simply fill out the form under the 'Calc Menu' link to calculate your calorie needs and receive personalized meal suggestions.

    Enjoy your culinary journey and happy cooking!
